Our Mission

Cannabis Intelligence for a Changing Landscape

The cannabis industry in the United States is about to face a seismic shift. Pending federal hemp legislation — expected to take effect in November 2025 — will prohibit cannabis seeds from crossing state lines. For growers, breeders, and consumers, this means one thing: you need to know what's available in your state, right now.

The Problem: Once the hemp law takes effect, the free flow of cannabis genetics across state borders ends. Growers will no longer be able to order seeds from out-of-state seed banks. The genetics available within your state's borders become your only legal options.

The Solution

CI Seed Map is the most comprehensive, independently researched directory of cannabis seed sellers in the United States. We've spent weeks manually verifying every breeder, seed bank, dispensary, and cultivator — documenting who they are, where they operate, what genetics they carry, and how to reach them.

Our map doesn't just show you where to buy seeds. It shows you the entire distribution network — which breeders are carried by which seed banks, which dispensaries stock which genetics, and where the physical storefronts are that you can walk into today.

What is Cannabis Intelligence?

Cannabis Intelligence (CI) is the practice of collecting, verifying, and organizing actionable data about the cannabis industry. It's not just a map — it's a living database that tracks the relationships between breeders, distributors, and retail locations.

In a post-hemp-law world, this intelligence becomes critical. Knowing that your local dispensary carries Compound Genetics or that a seed bank in your state stocks Mephisto Genetics isn't just convenient — it's the difference between having access to elite genetics and being locked out entirely.

Our Principles

Accuracy over speed. Every entry is manually researched and verified. We don't scrape — we investigate.

Grower-first design. The map is built for the person trying to find seeds, not for advertisers. No pop-ups, no paywalls on the map itself, no clutter.

Data protection. This registry represents hundreds of hours of original research. We protect it with time-limited sessions and API-level security so it can't be scraped and resold.

Community-driven growth. Users can submit new pins, corrections, and updates. The map gets better because the community makes it better.
